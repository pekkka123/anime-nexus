⚔️ Jujutsu Kaisen

Jujutsu Kaisen follows Yuji Itadori, a high school student who becomes the vessel of the King of Curses, Ryomen Sukuna. He joins Tokyo Jujutsu High to battle dangerous curses and protect humanity alongside powerful sorcerers.

Main Characters

Yuji Itadori

A kind-hearted teenager who becomes Sukuna’s vessel after swallowing a cursed object. Yuji possesses immense physical strength and fights to save others.

Nobara Kugisaki

A bold and fearless jujutsu sorcerer who uses straw doll techniques and cursed nails. She values confidence and individuality.

Megumi Fushiguro

A calm and strategic sorcerer who uses powerful shikigami summoned through the Ten Shadows Technique.

Satoru Gojo

The strongest jujutsu sorcerer alive, known for his Limitless technique and Six Eyes ability. He mentors Yuji and his classmates.

Ryomen Sukuna

The terrifying King of Curses whose immense power threatens both humans and sorcerers alike.

Kento Nanami

A calm and disciplined grade-one sorcerer who approaches combat with precision and professionalism.

Maki Zenin

Despite lacking cursed energy, Maki is an elite fighter who relies on her incredible strength and weapon mastery.

Toge Inumaki

A quiet sorcerer who uses cursed speech techniques capable of controlling enemies with his words.

Panda

A highly intelligent cursed corpse with immense physical strength and multiple combat forms.
