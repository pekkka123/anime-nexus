Jujutsu Kaisen

Jujutsu Kaisen follows Yuji Itadori, who becomes the vessel of Ryomen Sukuna and enters the world of jujutsu sorcerers to fight curses.

Character List

Yuji Itadori

Main protagonist and vessel of Sukuna.

Nobara Kugisaki

A bold and confident jujutsu sorcerer.

Megumi Fushiguro

Strategic fighter using shikigami techniques.

Satoru Gojo

Strongest jujutsu sorcerer alive.

Ryomen Sukuna

King of Curses.

Kento Nanami

Calm and disciplined sorcerer.

Maki Zenin

Fighter with no cursed energy but extreme strength.

Toge Inumaki

Uses cursed speech technique.

Panda

Cursed corpse with intelligence and strength.
