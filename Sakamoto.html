Sakamoto Days

Taro Sakamoto is the main protagonist of the series Sakamoto Days. Previously the best assassin in the world and a former member of the Order, he now runs a convenience store with his wife Aoi and daughter Hana.

Character List

Taro Sakamoto

Legendary ex-hitman who now lives peacefully running a convenience store with his family.

Lu Xiaotong

Daughter of a Triad family who now works at Sakamoto’s store and helps the team.

Shin Asakura

Telepathic former hitman who now supports Sakamoto’s family in their daily life.

Aoi Sakamoto

Sakamoto’s wife whose kindness helped him leave the assassin world behind.

Hana Sakamoto

Their daughter who brings joy and warmth into the Sakamoto family’s life.
