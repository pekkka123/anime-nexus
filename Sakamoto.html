🛒 Sakamoto Days

Sakamoto Days follows Taro Sakamoto, a legendary retired assassin who gave up his dangerous life to live peacefully with his family while managing a convenience store. However, his past continues to catch up with him as enemies and assassins appear.

Main Characters

Taro Sakamoto

A legendary former hitman feared by the underworld. He now lives peacefully with his family while protecting them from threats from his past.

Lu Xiaotong

Daughter of a powerful Triad family who joins Sakamoto’s convenience store and becomes part of the growing team.

Shin Asakura

A telepathic former assassin who deeply respects Sakamoto and assists him in protecting their peaceful life.

Aoi Sakamoto

Sakamoto’s loving wife whose kindness and support inspired him to leave behind the dangerous assassin lifestyle.

Hana Sakamoto

The cheerful daughter of the Sakamoto family who brings warmth and happiness into their everyday lives.
