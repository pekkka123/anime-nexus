🔥 Fire Force

Fire Force follows special firefighters who battle dangerous human combustion cases while uncovering the mystery behind spontaneous human ignition and the secrets of the Evangelist.

Main Characters

Shinra Kusakabe

Third-generation pyrokinetic known as “Devil’s Footprints” because of his flame-powered kicks. He aims to become a hero and uncover the truth behind his family’s tragedy.

Arthur Boyle

A delusional knight-themed pyrokinetic who wields a plasma sword called Excalibur. Despite his strange personality, he is one of Company 8’s strongest fighters.

Maki Oze

A powerful second-generation pyrokinetic and former military soldier. She combines incredible physical strength with advanced flame control abilities.

Iris

A kind-hearted sister from the Holy Sol Temple who prays for Infernals before they are purified. She supports Company 8 emotionally and spiritually.

Akitaru Obi

The fearless captain of Special Fire Force Company 8. Although he lacks pyrokinetic powers, his leadership and physical strength inspire the entire team.
